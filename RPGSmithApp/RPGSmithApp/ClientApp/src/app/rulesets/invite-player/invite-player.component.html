Invite Player
{{rulesetModel.ruleSetName}}
To invite a player to join this campaign provide either the Player's RPGSmith Account Username, or a valid email address.If the recipient does not have a RPGSmith account they will receive an email prompting them to create one to join this campaign.

You can rescind a player invite from the Campaign Management Interface by selecting the manage button (ellipsis) on the corresponding Player tile and selecting Cancel Invite.

To remove a joined Player from your campaign, simply delete their character, this will free up the Player slot which can be used to send another invite to this or a different player. Additional Player slots can be purchased to allow for more players to join your campaign.
